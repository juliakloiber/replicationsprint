Defining the product to replicate



Research on the audience, the thematic focus, the features and challenges. Asking questions like: What does it do? Why is it important?, Who are the users and how do they interact with the product?, ...

Documentation: Before the sprint, the Core Team will be running surveys, talking to Product Owners and Experts, collecting information, and archiving everything for the sprint in open formats.







"Good preparation is crucial. The better a sprint is prepared, the more successful it will be. This might sound obvious, but the sprints are unusually context-dependant. Like a classical concert, the sprint is the live premiere, a culmination of meticulous and comprehensive training."
