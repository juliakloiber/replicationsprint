Getting the band together



Putting together a team of experts, mentors and facilitators.







Instead of focusing on people, we will initially focus on types of expertise. “UX design”, “relational database development”, “front-end dev” etc. This will give us an overview of what is needed to get the project done. From there, we can start sourcing participants that cover one or more types of expertise.
